About Evergreen High

Discover our mission, history, staff, and values.

Our Mission

At Evergreen High, our mission is to cultivate a nurturing and challenging educational environment that empowers students to achieve their full potential. We are committed to fostering intellectual curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning. Our goal is to prepare students to be responsible, ethical, and engaged global citizens who contribute positively to their communities.

Our History

Founded in 1985, Evergreen High has a rich history of academic excellence and community involvement. What began as a small school with a handful of students has grown into a thriving institution known for its innovative teaching methods and commitment to student success. Over the years, we have expanded our programs, facilities, and outreach efforts to meet the evolving needs of our diverse student body.

Our Staff

Our dedicated team of educators and staff are the heart of Evergreen High. We employ experienced and passionate professionals who are committed to providing a supportive and stimulating learning environment. Our faculty members are experts in their fields, and they work collaboratively to ensure that every student receives the personalized attention and guidance they need to succeed.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, ethics, and responsibility in our actions.
  • Respect: We foster a culture of mutual respect and understanding.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and approaches to enhance learning.
  • Community: We build strong relationships among students, staff, and families.
